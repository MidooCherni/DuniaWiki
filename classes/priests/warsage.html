Classes > Priests > Warsage

Non-Lawful, non-Evil only. A fierce and zealous servant of the War God who lives to further the eternal crusade of the Dragons by channeling bloodlust through themselves and others. They all speak Draconic.

Warsages are hardy and steeled for battle. Whether for honorable reasons or for mere pillage, they live from conflict to conflict, and constantly prepare themselves for skirmishes. They use their bodies as conduits for the primal spirits of warfare from an ancient time in Dunian history, and are quick to use this power to snap into martial rage.

Warpriests add their CHA modifier to all of their damaging miracles. They are barred from the Frost school of magic. They can sense any raging or hastened creature nearby and read its mind, and can consume their own turn to extend this effect for one more turn once per target.

Level 1: Warsages can channel bloodlust once per day per 6 levels to grant a creature they can see +10 to their next attack roll or an extra attack (whichever they choose.)

Level 3: Warsages can innately cast Spiritual Weapon without memorization. When using their spirit weapon, it glows violently like fire, and allows them to doublestrike every turn as well as add their level to both their damage rolls instead of the usual Priest half level.

Level 5: Warsages can add an extra +2 points to their attack rolls and double their land speed if they can smell blood (any enemy unit has less health points than its maximum.)

Level 10: Bloodlust also grants the target resistance (halve incoming damage) for one turn. The blood scent bonus increases to +5

Level 15: Bloodlust becomes a group spell that targets all allies within hearing range. The blood scent bonus increases again to +10

Classes > Knights > Holy Inquisitor

Lawful Human only. Also known as Holy Knight, White Knight, Paladin, Crusader or Zealot. A fervent cavalier in service of the Hylonian Empire's Church of Light or any of its equivalents, blessed with the ability to wield searing divine fire and healing light.

Their code of honor consists of speaking the absolute truth, never cooperating with the Dragons, and eradicating all Demonic and Undead creatures.

Inquisitors must travel to the Hylonian Kingdom's capital for their promotion. They must seek out a Bishop of the Light for forgiveness if they ever break their oath. Any bonus damage they deal on their melee attacks can be dealt in the form of Holy or Fire magic damage at will.

Level 2: Inquisitors may cast spells as a Bishop of half their level using the CHA stat.

Level 5: Inquisitors may compel any creature capable of speech to speak the truth by reaching with their outstretched hand. They may do this once per day for every 2 points of Charisma they have above 10. They also gain the ability to sense holy and unholy things within 100 feet of them, such as the presence of divine creatures (but not their identity), water, and consecrated or desecrated land.

Level 10: Inquisitors and their allies become immune to fear as long as the Inquisitor mintains concentration. Inquisitors gain advantage on Knowledge checks about religion.

Level 15: Inquisitors gain the ability to beam sunfire from their bodies once daily, blinding any non-human creatures for one round. Draconic, Demonic and Undead creatures further receive 1d10 Holy damage for every 2 levels of the Inquisitor rounded down.

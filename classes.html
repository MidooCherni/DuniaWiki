Classes

Dunia's 7 playable archetypes include a total of 30 classes:

  • Warrior: Fighter, Savage, Monk, Spellsword
  • Knight: Inquisitor, Templar, Draconic, Moon Knight, Reaper, Stormlord
  • Mage: Wizard, Witch, Sorcerer, Necromancer
  • Rogue: Thief, Assassin, Pirate, Bard
  • Ranger: Marksman, Beastlord, Lacerator, Engineer
  • Priest: Bishop, Cultist, Warsage, Druid
  • Civilian: Archeologist, Artisan, Healer, Merchant

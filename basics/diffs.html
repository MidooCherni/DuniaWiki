What's new in DUNIA?

Coming from D&D or Pathfinder? Here's a complete list of all the things in DUNIA that are explicitly different:

  • The Agility (AGI) stat is used to dodge attacks instead of AC. It didn't make sense to me an armored behemoth and a flighty little monk used the same mechanic for damage mitigation.
  • "Armor" instead reduces physical damage taken. You will be able to hit a slow, lumbering target, it's just not guaranteed you'll be hurting them.
  • Dexterity (DEX) modifies ALL attack chance rolls. Now you can be strong and clumsy, or a venomous little snake- rejoice!
  • Playable races have different dietary needs.
  • Experience points are simplified.
  • 30 playable classes, including non-combat civilians.
  • Cantrips are gone, as they trivialized survival and made magic feel petty and ubiquitous. Mages aren't superhuman; bending reality is a tiring and unique process that should be saved up for when you're REALLY in a bind.
  • Different spells, with many new additions.
  • A unique world inspired not just by the Forgotten Realms, but by other great media as well, such as Homestuck and Touhou.
  • A different creature classification system.
  • Optional procedurally generated side dungeons with loot formulas to make money and power up.
  • Player housing.

Classes > Knights > Draconic Warlord

Also known as Red Knight, Dragon Knight, Commander or Battlelord. A commander of war blessed by the Dragon God who embodies the regal rage of the aspects, manifesting wings for flight or combat and elemental breath. They represent the true spirit of combat and the natural justice of the feral world, relishing in blood-spilling and the thrill of honorable struggle.

Despite their bloodlust, which attracts ancient Draconic spirits all the way from Wrath'mar to magically empower them, their code emphasizes a fight is only ever as honorable as it is fair, as well as the importance of seeking out worthy foes. They are forbidden from attacking disabled enemies (asleep, charmed, stunnedâ€¦) and must seek out Warsages in Dren'nin if they ever break their oath.

They are fluent in Draconic, and have advantage on Intimidation.

Level 2: The Draconic's skin begins to toughen, hardening not unlike the scales of a Dragonkin. Enemies attacking them deal damage with disadvantage.

Level 5: Draconics may fire blast once per three levels daily rounded down. They also gain the ability to direct allied units, spending one action to allow an ally an extra action.

Level 7: Draconics may sprout wings and fly for up to an hour total once per day.

Level 10: Draconics may roar fiercely once per 5 levels daily, mimicking the fear spell and allowing every ally under the effect of charm, sleep or fear to snap awake.

Level 15: Draconics gain the ability to transform into their true form once per day, shapeshifting into a small Dragon, gaining complete immunity to fire and a reduction of all incoming damage by 10 points for 5 rounds. They also gain a rallying cry which returns any allies who have fallen into a dying state back to 1 hit point.

Level 17: The Draconic and all their allies roll for initiative with advantage.

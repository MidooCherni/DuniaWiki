Attributes

Attribute scores, commonly called stats, have different uses:
  • Strength: Carry weight (STR*6 lbs), bonus damage, gear requirement, athleticism
  • Constitution: Health, resistance to natural conditions and elements, concentration
  • Dexterity: Hit accuracy bonus, a wide array of skills
  • Agility: Dodge chance, 2ft/sec base movement speed per point, turn order bonus
  • Intellect: Mage spell slots, mana pool size, pure knowledge checks
  • Wisdom: Priest spell slots, magical resistance
  • Charisma: Social skill checks

An attribute's modifier is equal to +/-1 for every 2 points above or below 10 respectively. It is added to certain rolls explicitly when needed. If you are asked roll (attribute) DC X or (skill) DC X where X is a number, you roll a d20 dice and add your attribute modifier or your skill value to the roll, and if it is higher than X, you succeed.

Classes > Knights > Death Reaper

Non-Lawful, Evil only. Also known as Death Knight, Rider or Cavalreaper. A servant of pure death and dread who makes use of forbidden necromantic power in armed combat and psychic terror to cripple enemies. Dragonkin, Sun Elves, Fey Elves and Lunarians cannot be Reapers.

As historical enemies of Moon Knights, Reapers are agents of the spirits of Rot and Decay and sworn jailors of the living. Their cult is dedicated to trapping the souls of the dead in shambling coils of unlife, growing the power of their own armies by controlling masses of unholy creatures to further their aims of turning Dunia into an extension of the lower planes. Once engaged in combat, they are forbidden from leaving their victim alive, except for the express intent of torturing them.

Level 2: Death Reapers may cast the spells of a Necromancer of half their level.

Level 5: Death Reapers may harm-touch a living creature once per 5 levels, dealing 1d6 Shadow damage per 2 levels.

Level 10: A Death Reaper may put together a deathsteed out of the remains of slain warhorses, an unquestioningly loyal magical horse that will carry them without tiring and charge without hesitation. They also gain an oppressive aura of undeath that slowly wilts and discolors all plant life around them.

Level 15: A Death Reaper gains the right to wield a runeblade, converting a normal 1d8 Longsword into a +3 magical weapon that heals the wielder for the damage dealt on critical strikes. Anyone attempting to wield the weapon other than its master is painfully seared for its damage value. At level 20, runeblades also trap the souls of their victims, allowing the Reaper to read their memories.
